**DGT**

This folder contains 4 functions for DGT/invDGT used in the training process.

'calcCanonicalDualWindow.m' is from https://doi.org/10/c3qb under the MIT license.
Please refer to the paper,
Kohei Yatabe, Yoshiki Masuyama, Tsubasa Kusano and Yasuhiro Oikawa, "Representation of complex spectrogram via phase conversion," 
Acoustical Science and Technology, vol.40, no.3, May 2019. (Open Access),
for more detailed information of the whole toolbox.
